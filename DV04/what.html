RIVER CROSSING: I WANT TO COMMUNICATE WITH YOU is the opportunity to nominate someone to be commemorated by having the public dock in Sacramento or West Sacramento named after them.


Nominate Someone


Anyone from anywhere in the world can suggest the name of someone meaningful to them but we are particularly interested to receive nominations that have a connection to the cities of Sacramento and West Sacramento.


As well as letting us know the name of the person you have chosen, you should tell us why you are nominating them in up to 250 words. It could be your beloved grandma, your best school friend, a neighbor; it could even be you!


After the competition closes on 10th July 2019, a panel of judges will choose 2 winners from the names and reasons submitted.
