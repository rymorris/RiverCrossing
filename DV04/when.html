The competition to find two people whose names will become that of the public dock in Sacramento or West Sacramento opened on 25th May 2019. Submissions will close on 10th July 2019.


Nominate Someone


During this time you can send us the name of the person you wish to nominate. As well as letting us know the name you propose, you should tell us why in up to 250 words.


We want to build up a
biography of the people of
Sacramento and West Sacramento.

After the call for nominations closes, the judges will select two names to be commemorated. One name will be chosen for the public dock in Sacramento and the other for the public dock in West Sacramento.


The public docks will be newly named in the Fall of 2019.
